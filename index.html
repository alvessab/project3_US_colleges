Project By: Sabrina Alves, Nicklaus Austin, Cody Gunter


This site is for early college research. Because future salary is a significant consideration in college and major decision making, we have provided two dashboards to start narrowing down what you want to do and where you want to go: "Salary vs Degree" and "Salary vs Type and Region". Have other criteria in mind? Use the map to start getting the names of colleges in the United States. Make sure to click on the pins; they have links to the school for your consideration!
